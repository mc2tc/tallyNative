# Premium Modules Drawer Organization Summary

## Overview
The Premium Modules drawer has been reorganized into logical categories to improve navigation and user experience.

## Category Structure

### 1. **Operations**
Core business operations and inventory management:
- **Inventory** - Track items as they're consumed (for resale or as ingredients)
- **Production** - Group ingredients to make products
- **Point of Sale (POS)** - System for selling resell items and products

### 2. **Sales & Marketing**
Customer acquisition and sales management:
- **CRM** - Sales pipeline and customer relationship management
- **Invoicing** - Create and manage invoices for customers
- **Online Sales** - Dedicated URL for online sales
- **Online Booking** - Appointment scheduling and reservations

### 3. **People**
Human resources and workforce management:
- **Payroll** - Employee payroll processing and management
- **Expenses** - Track and manage employee expenses
- **Time Management** - Employee time tracking and attendance

### 4. **Tally Network**
Supplier network and financial services:
- **Suppliers** - Network of suppliers offering discounted prices (Tally Network)
- **Financial Services** - Various financing options (invoice financing, loans, etc.)

### 5. **Tax & Compliance**
Regulatory compliance and reporting:
- **Year End Reporting** - Financial reports for authorities and tax preparation

## Notes
- Accounting & Bookkeeping remain the core of the app (not in Premium Modules)
- All modules are accessible directly from the drawer
- Each category groups related functionality for easier discovery
